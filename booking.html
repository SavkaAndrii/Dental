F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we work with most major international and national insurance providers. Prior to your first visit, you can submit your policy details to our receptionist for coverage pre-approval.

Are you accepting new patients?

Absolutely! We are always happy to welcome new patients of all ages. Your first visit typically includes a comprehensive 3D dental scan, thorough consultation, and a custom treatment plan.

How often should I get a professional cleaning?

We recommend scheduling a professional oral hygiene cleaning every 6 months. This helps prevent plaque buildup, cavities, and gum disease, while maintaining the natural brightness of your teeth.

Do you offer flexible payment plans?

Yes, we understand that investing in your smile is important. Our clinic provides flexible payment options and installment plans in partnership with leading financial institutions for major treatments and implants.
